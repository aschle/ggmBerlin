About

GeekGirlMeetup is free un-conference on web, start-up and code. It's a one day event, and the attendees are the programme. Everyone can contribute and volunteer to get involved!

Angela Naegele
@anschieN
Angela has worked over 4 years as a Visual and UX Designer. At Nokia she focuses on concepting and designing mobile applications and has learnt to challenge technical restrictions faced by design. She enjoys working in an agile and intercultural environment. Angela holds a degree in Information Design from the University of Applied Sciences Schwäbisch Gmünd and is currently doing her Master's degree in Business and Leadership at the University of Arts Berlin and University of St. Gallen.
How to Design for Mobile
The session gives a short overview of the evolution of mobile UI and focuses on new challenges around designing rich applications for smaller screens as well as having a user centric design approach. It outlines the design process and showcases some personal work examples. The talk is directed to everyone who wants to get an insight in a mobile design approach.
